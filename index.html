I am a CS student at IIIT Hyderabad and also a member of Prof. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru "PK"s research group, Precog. My primary interest lies in the application of geometric deep learning to social network data. In general, I'm interested in exploring problem statements concerning any kind of network data.

In the summer of 2023, I interned at the Applied AI/ML team of JPMorgan's Corporate Investment Banking division working on a tool to mine insights for root cause analysis from large volumes of transactions. In the summer of 2022, I worked under Prof. Hong Cheng at the Chinese University of Hong Kong on dynamic community detection on temporal networks, more specifically, real-time modularity maximization using streaming data structures.

In my free time, I explore the intersection of applied mathematics and GNNs, and catching up on latest works in the domain. Feel free to look at my CV or to reach out to me!
