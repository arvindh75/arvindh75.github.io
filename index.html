I am a CS student at IIIT Hyderabad and also a member of Prof. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru "PK"s research group, Precog. My interests lie in the applications of geometric deep learning to social network data.

In the summer of 2023, I interned at the Applied AI/ML team of JPMorgan's Corporate Investment Banking division working on mining insights from large volumes of transactions. In the previous summer, I worked with Prof. Hong Cheng at the Chinese University of Hong Kong on dynamic community detection on temporal networks.
